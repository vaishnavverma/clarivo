# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Clarivo AI

## 1. What is Clarivo AI?
Clarivo AI is an AI-powered platform designed to help students get accurate and easy-to-understand answers to their questions. It supports multiple subjects and topics, making learning faster and easier.

---

## 2. How do I use Clarivo AI?
1. Open the website in your browser.
2. Type your question into the input box.
3. Select the subject (optional) for more precise answers.
4. Press "Ask" and get an AI-generated response instantly.

---

## 3. Is Clarivo AI free to use?
Yes! Clarivo AI is completely free for students. Some advanced features may be added in the future, but the core AI answering system will remain free.

---

## 4. Does Clarivo AI collect my personal data?
No. Clarivo AI respects your privacy:
- We do not sell or share your data.
- We only process the questions you type to generate answers.
- Optional email or contact info is used only if you reach out for support.

---

## 5. Can I contribute to Clarivo AI?
Absolutely! Check out the [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) file for guidelines on how to fork, make changes, and submit pull requests.

---

## 6. Can I deploy Clarivo AI myself?
Yes! You can clone this repository and run it locally. Follow the instructions in [README.md](README.md) under "Installation" to set it up.
